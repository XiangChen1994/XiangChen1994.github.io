Biography

Xiang Chen is currently a second year postgraduate student in College of Electronics and Information Engineering at Sichuan University, under the supervision of Prof. Xiaohai He. He earned his B. Eng degree from Sichuan University (SCU) in 2016, where he worked with Prof. Xiaohai He.

He is interested in developing algorithms and theories in deep learning/machine learning, image processing, especially computer vision.
